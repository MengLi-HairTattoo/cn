About

Our Studio | Meng Li Hair & Tattoo

This page builds trust: our principles, hygiene & safety, workflow, and why we’re worth booking.

Meng Li is a professional creative studio combining barbering and tattooing. We believe true style comes from years of accumulated technique, aesthetics, and respect for detail—not short-lived trends.

Here, hair and tattoos aren’t just services—they’re two crafts that demand focus, patience, and responsibility.

Our philosophy

  • • Meng Li is built on three principles: professionalism, restraint, and the long term.
  • • We respect every guest’s body and image. We focus on structure, proportion, and wearability—never superficial hype or over-packaged styles.
  • • Whether hair or tattoos, we judge by one standard: will it stand the test of time? So your work keeps its value over the years.

Why Malaysia?

  • • Malaysia is a multicultural place—open to different backgrounds, styles, and forms of creation.
  • • For us, this is more than a place to work—it’s a space where true craft is understood and respected.
  • • We aim to bring years of craft and experience to Southeast Asia—building a creative space that connects Asia with an international perspective, and returning hair and tattooing to their professional roots.

Standards

Professional • Restraint • Long-term

We judge by one standard—whether it lasts. We bring craft and aesthetics into every detail.

Safety & Hygiene

Safety and hygiene are Meng Li’s most basic—and most important—principles.

  • • Needles and consumables used during tattooing are single-use and disposable.
  • • All equipment follows professional sterilization procedures.
  • • Work areas are cleaned and disinfected regularly to ensure a safe environment.
  • • Hair and tattoo areas are separated to avoid cross-contamination.
  • • We treat every guest with professional standards—and take responsibility for every piece we create.
